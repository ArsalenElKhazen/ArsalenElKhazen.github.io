What do bodybuilders use to build muscle?

Equipment & Gear includes tools bodybuilders use for training and safety. Weights and machines like dumbbells and barbells build strength, while resistance bands add tension. Lifting straps, gloves, and belts improve grip and stability, and knee/elbow sleeves support joints.
These items enhance performance and reduce injury risk.

  1. Weights & Machines

    2. Weights & Machines are tools used in strength training to build muscle and increase strength. Weights, such as dumbbells and barbells, provide resistance that the muscles work against during exercises. Machines are equipment that guide the movement and target specific muscle groups,
    offering controlled resistance and helping ensure proper form. Both are essential for progressive overload, which is key to muscle growth.

  2. Resistance Bands

    3. Resistance Bands are flexible, stretchable bands used in strength training to provide adjustable resistance during exercises. They come in different levels of tension and are often used for mobility work, warming up, or adding extra resistance to exercises like squats, lunges, and push-ups.
    They are portable, affordable, and can be used to target specific muscle groups.

  3. Gym Accessories

    4. Gym Accessories are additional tools and equipment used to enhance workout performance, comfort, and safety. They include items like lifting straps, gloves, belts, and knee sleeves, which help improve grip, provide support, and prevent injury These accessories complement workouts by
    offering extra functionality and protection.
