The importance of food for bodybuilders

Food is essential for bodybuilding, supporting muscle growth, recovery, and performance. Protein aids muscle repair, while carbs provide energy and prevent breakdown. A balanced diet helps burn fat, regulates metabolism, and reduces injury risk. Proper nutrition also maintains hormone levels, boosting strength and endurance. A well-structured diet maximizes results.
