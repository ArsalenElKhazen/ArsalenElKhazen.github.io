Which hormones assist muscle growth in bodybuilders?

Hormones for bodybuilders are naturally occurring or synthetic substances that help regulate processes like muscle growth, strength, recovery, and fat loss. They influence key functions such as protein synthesis, metabolism, and energy use, which are critical for improving performance and enhancing physique.

  1. Testosterone

    2. Essential for muscle growth, strength, and recovery.

  2. Human Growth Hormone (HGH)

    3. Promotes muscle growth, fat loss, and recovery.

  3. Insulin

    4. Helps with nutrient uptake and muscle repair.

  4. Insulin-Like Growth Factor 1 (IGF-1)

    5. Stimulates muscle growth and repair.

  5. Cortisol

    6. A stress hormone; managing it helps prevent muscle breakdown.
